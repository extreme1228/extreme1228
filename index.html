What I do

I explore how agentic systems can learn and generalize with minimal task-specific supervision. My recent work focuses on GUI agents, reinforcement learning, and scalable multi-task frameworks.

This site is a living notebook: a place to share ideas, summaries, and research progress.

Highlights

Research

Current directions

GUI Agents

Understanding how agents perceive and act in interfaces, and how to evaluate their generalization across tasks.

Task-Free Learning

Designing frameworks that learn behaviors without explicit task labels, enabling scalable agent training.

RL Generalization

Building separated and modular models to improve transfer in visually complex environments.
